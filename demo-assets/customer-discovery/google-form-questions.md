# MindCraft Showcase Discovery Form

Use this as the exact Google Form copy. Keep the form short and label it as a 2-minute parent signal check.

## Form Title

MindCraft Parent Signal Check

## Form Description

We are learning what families need most when maths starts feeling stressful. This takes about 2 minutes. Your answers help us build MindCraft around real parent and student needs.

## Questions

### 1. Which best describes you?
Type: Multiple choice
- Parent / guardian
- Student
- Educator
- Tutor / learning coach
- Founder / builder
- Other

### 2. What grade range is most relevant to you?
Type: Checkboxes
- Middle school
- 9th-10th grade
- 11th-12th grade
- ACT / SAT prep
- College
- Not applicable

### 3. What feels hardest when a student struggles in maths?
Type: Checkboxes
Help text: Pick up to 2.
- Knowing what they actually do not understand
- Getting them to practice consistently
- Homework turning into conflict at home
- ACT / test prep feeling urgent
- Tutoring not feeling connected to what they need
- Worrying they are using AI without learning
- Finding help I can trust

### 4. When your child gets stuck, what usually happens?
Type: Multiple choice
- They keep trying and ask for help
- They avoid it or shut down
- A parent tries to help, but it gets stressful
- We search online / use AI / watch videos
- We use a tutor, but the tutor has to diagnose from scratch
- Not applicable

### 5. What would make you trust a premium learning platform?
Type: Checkboxes
Help text: Pick up to 3.
- A clear diagnostic map of strengths and gaps
- Proof that practice is improving mastery over time
- Tutor credentials and human support
- Parent dashboard that is simple to understand
- Testimonials from families
- School / educator partnerships
- A short trial or live demo
- Transparent pricing and cancellation
- Strong data privacy for students

### 6. Which MindCraft idea feels most valuable?
Type: Checkboxes
Help text: Pick up to 2.
- See where the struggle starts
- Know the next right skill to practice
- Homework help that explains, not just answers
- Tutoring with context before the session starts
- Parent visibility without micromanaging
- Students studying together with guardrails
- AI tools that build thinking instead of replacing it

### 7. If MindCraft clearly helped your student make progress, what monthly price would feel reasonable?
Type: Multiple choice
- I would want a free trial first
- Under $100/month
- $100-$160/month
- $160-$220/month
- $220+/month if tutoring is included
- I would rather pay per session
- Not sure yet

### 8. When would this matter most?
Type: Multiple choice
- Right now
- Before the next ACT / major test
- This semester
- Before next school year
- I am mostly curious

### 9. What would you want to see before trusting MindCraft?
Type: Paragraph
Help text: One sentence is enough.

### 10. Want us to follow up?
Type: Short answer
Help text: Optional. Leave name + email or phone.
