FAQs

How is MindCraft different from regular tutoring?

Regular tutoring helps you in the moment and then disappears. MindCraft turns every session into something you can build on. Notes get organized automatically, progress is tracked over time, and JARVIS, our AI assistant, keeps you moving forward even between sessions.

What subjects can I get help with?

We cover Intro to Algorithmic Trading, Accounting Basics, Networking, Style, and more. We are growing fast. If your subject is not listed, reach out and we will find the right tutor for you.

What actually happens during a session?

You meet your tutor live and work through exactly what you need. After the session, everything gets turned into a clear summary with key takeaways and next steps that show up right on your dashboard.

How are tutors chosen?

Every MindCraft tutor is a high-performing college student who recently mastered what they teach. They know exactly where students get stuck because they just went through it themselves. We vet each one personally.

Is there any commitment?

None at all. Your first session is completely free. After that, book when it works for you. No contracts, no packages, no pressure.

What does my child get after each session?

A structured summary with what was covered, clear takeaways, and personalized next steps. It lives on their dashboard so nothing gets lost or forgotten. Parents can see it too.

Can I track my child's progress?

Yes. The platform tracks what your student understands, where they need reinforcement, and how they are growing session by session. You no longer have to guess what is going on with their learning.

I'm a student. Can I sign up without my parents?

Absolutely. MindCraft is built for students who want to take ownership of their learning. Book your own session, get your dashboard, and start building.

How do I become a MindCraft tutor?

We look for college students who are strong in their subject and genuinely enjoy teaching. If that sounds like you, reach out through the site. We will review your application and get back to you.

Where do sessions take place?

Everything is online, through our platform. No commuting, no logistics. Join from anywhere on any device.
