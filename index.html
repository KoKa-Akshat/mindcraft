FAQs

What does MindCraft actually do for my child?

MindCraft first diagnoses exactly what your child doesn't understand, then helps them fix it. With a real human tutor and mathematical tools we keep them moving forward. With confidence.

Who teaches my child?

Our tutors are high-performing college students who recently mastered these exact subjects and exams. They know where students get stuck because they just went through it. We vet and train each one personally.

What exams and subjects do you cover?

We specialise in teaching maths including ACT, SAT, IB, and high school exams, as well as general school work help. If there is a new course or subject you want to study, we can create a custom plan for you.

How will I know if it's working?

After every session your child receives a breakdown of what was covered and what is next. You can see their knowledge map update in real time and watch gaps close over time. No more guessing.

What happens during a session?

Your child meets their tutor live, works through the exact concepts they are stuck on, and leaves with a clear plan. Notes and next steps are posted to their dashboard automatically after every session.

Is it safe and monitored?

All sessions take place on our secure platform. We personally vet every tutor and monitor all interactions. Your child's safety and privacy come first, always.

How much does it cost?

Every feature on the app is free while we are in our testing phase. When you are ready for 1-on-1 tutoring, the first session is always on us. No credit card needed to start.

Is there a contract or commitment?

None at all. No contracts, no packages, no monthly fees. Book sessions when they work for your family. Cancel anytime, no questions asked.

My child is really behind. Will this work?

That is exactly who we are built for. MindCraft finds the root gaps, not just the surface symptoms, and rebuilds from there. Most students feel a real difference within the first two sessions.

How do we get started?

Create a free account, run the diagnostic, and see what MindCraft finds. If you want to book a tutor after, the first session is free. No commitment needed to try.
