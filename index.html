Frequently asked

What parents ask first.

A calmer product should also be easy to understand.

Is this tutoring or software? +

Both — and they work together. MindCraft gives students an AI-powered learning path and gives tutors the real-time data to help faster and more precisely. The AI handles the diagnosis and mapping; the tutor handles the human connection and explanation. Neither works as well without the other.

What grade levels and subjects does it support? +

The core experience is built around math — grade 6 through 12 and ACT exam prep. The knowledge map covers 42 concepts from foundational arithmetic through precalculus, all linked to real exam patterns. Middle school students get the same personalized map, without any ACT pressure — just clear, grade-appropriate math skill building. Subject expansion is on the roadmap.

How long until my child sees results? +

Most students notice a shift in confidence within 3–4 weeks. The knowledge map starts improving from the very first session — but the compound effect of practice data building on tutor sessions is what creates lasting change. Plan for a semester, celebrate the early wins.

Does my child need to be struggling? +

No. High-performing students use MindCraft to find the blind spots that don't show up until test day. The diagnostic is accurate even when a student scores well on class tests — it reveals the gaps that haven't been tested yet, and the thinking patterns that are quietly fragile.

Does it replace a tutor? +

No. It removes the guesswork so tutoring time is more focused, more efficient, and more confidence-building. The AI builds the map; the tutor navigates it with your child. The human relationship is irreplaceable — we just make sure every minute of it counts.
