FAQs

How is MindCraft different from regular tutoring?

MindCraft turns every session into structured learning your child can build on. We don't just help in the moment — we organize, reinforce, and extend what they learn so progress continues between sessions.

What actually happens in a session?

Students meet their tutor live, work through concepts, and build understanding step by step. After the session, everything is organized into clear notes and next steps so they know exactly what to review and practice.

Will this help my child become more independent?

Yes. Our goal is to help students rely less on tutors over time. Each session builds habits around thinking, reviewing, and practicing so they can move forward on their own.

How are tutors selected?

Our tutors are high-performing college students who recently mastered these subjects themselves. They understand exactly where students struggle and how to explain concepts clearly.

How do sessions fit into a busy schedule?

Sessions are flexible and scheduled around your availability. Most families choose one or two sessions per week depending on their needs.

Where do sessions take place?

Sessions are held live online through our platform, so students can join from anywhere.

What does my child get after each session?

Students leave with structured notes, clear takeaways, and personalized practice so they can keep improving between sessions.

How do you track progress?

We track what students understand, where they need reinforcement, and what they should focus on next. Progress becomes clear over time.

Is there a commitment required?

No long-term commitment. You can start with a free session and continue based on what works best for your child.

How do I get started?

Simply request a session through the site. We'll match your child with the right tutor and get them started.
