## Files

- `google-form-questions.md` - copy deck for a manual Google Form.

- `mindcraft_parent_signal_check_printable.pdf` - print this for paper responses.
- `mindcraft_parent_signal_check_printable.png` - image preview / Canva import.

## Fast Google Form Setup

1. Go to `https://script.google.com/home`.
2. Create a new project.
3. Paste the full contents of `create-google-form.gs`.
4. Run `createMindCraftParentSignalForm`.
5. Approve Google permissions.
6. Open `View > Logs` and copy the `Live form URL`.
7. Make a QR code from that live URL in Canva or any QR tool.
8. Put the QR beside the booth with this label:

`2-minute parent signal check`

`Help us build the version of MindCraft families actually need.`

## Paper Print Setup

Print `mindcraft_parent_signal_check_printable.pdf`.

Recommended quantity: 40-60 copies.

Recommended print settings:

- Paper: US Letter, 8.5 x 11
- Color: Full color
- Sides: Single-sided
- Scale: Fit to printable area
- Paper weight: Standard or 24 lb

Put these at the booth with pens and a small drop area.

## Booth Ask

`Could you give us one parent signal? It takes about 2 minutes and helps us learn what families need most when maths gets stressful.`

## What To Look For After Showcase

Strong buying signal:

- They pick `Right now` or `Before the next ACT / major test`.
- They choose `Clear diagnostic map`, `Proof of progress over time`, or `Human tutor support`.
- They pick `$160-$220/mo` or `$220+/mo with tutoring`.
- They leave contact info.

Strong product signal:

- They mention trust, visibility, homework conflict, ACT urgency, or not knowing what to fix next.
- They ask how tutoring, AI, and parent reporting connect.
- They ask whether their child can use it on an iPad.

Follow-up line:

`Thank you for sharing this. We are talking to a small group of families after Showcase so we can build the right version first. Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week?`
