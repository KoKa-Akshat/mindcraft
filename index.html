Founder · Math learning

Akshat Koirala

Math and economics at Macalester. Eight years helping students find the first step that finally makes sense.

Click portrait
MathTutoringMentorship

Akshat has taught math across Nepal, South Africa, and the United States. At Macalester, he has worked as a statistics preceptor and peer tutor. The pattern was always the same: students did not need louder explanations. They needed the right starting point.

MindCraft is that instinct turned into a system. Find the break. Name the next move. Let confidence return through work the student can actually do.

Founder · Product + data

Blake Kell

Data science and math. Builds the calm interface, learning signals, and product feel behind MindCraft.

Click portrait
DataDesignFocus

Blake builds the parts that make MindCraft feel clear: the data flow, the interface, and the small signals that tell a student, "you are here."

His work is personal. Learning with ADHD or neurodivergence should not feel like being the problem. The tool should lower the noise and make progress visible.
