## What To Order

Cards:
- Order 50 cards, even though you need 44. This leaves room for test prints, damaged cards, and people taking extras.
- Product: double-sided business cards.
- Final trim size: 3.5 in x 2 in.
- Artwork size supplied: 3.75 in x 2.25 in with 0.125 in bleed on every edge.
- Finish: matte or soft-touch if available. Avoid glossy because QR glare can make scanning worse.
- Paper: premium/heavier card stock if turnaround allows.

Poster:
- Order 1-2 posters.
- Best: 18 in x 24 in mounted poster/foam board for the booth.
- Backup/same-day: 11 in x 17 in poster print on heavyweight matte paper.
- Finish: matte. Avoid glossy if the QR will sit under event lighting.

## What To Tell FedEx

Use this wording:

> I have print-ready 300 dpi PDFs. Please print the business cards double-sided, full color, trim to 3.5 by 2 inches, and use the included bleed. Please do not scale, stretch, add borders, or fit-to-page. For the poster, please print at actual size, full color, matte finish, and keep the QR code sharp.

Before paying, ask:

> Can you show me the print preview and confirm no text or QR code is getting cut off?

## Canva Route

If Canva is easier:

1. Create a custom design at `3.75 in x 2.25 in` for each card side.
2. Upload the card PNG.
3. Stretch it exactly to the full canvas edge.
4. Download as `PDF Print`.
5. Do not add Canva crop marks if FedEx is already trimming from the bleed file.

For posters:

1. Create custom design at `18 in x 24 in` or `11 in x 17 in`.
2. Upload the matching poster PNG.
3. Stretch to full canvas.
4. Export as `PDF Print`.
